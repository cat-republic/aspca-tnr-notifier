Schedule Services

For self-transport appointments at the Glendale clinic and ASPCA transport appointments, click on calendar item to make a reservation for that date. To view a map of the transport pickup locations for the month, please click here. Please be aware that some transport dates that have already been filled may still show on the map. (Please note that transport appointments include the spay/neuter appointment and it is not necessary to schedule both.)

Only available appointments will display, so if there are no events listed on a date, that means that all appointments for that day have been filled.

Note: This calendar only displays self-transport appointments at the Glendale clinic and ASPCA transport appointments. For all trap bank reservations, click here. 

 

Rescuers who are not within the same organization may authorize another rescuer to pick up their animals on an ongoing basis by signing a release listing other rescuers who are authorized to pick up for them. The rescuer picking up must have a current signed ASPCA Terms of Use Agreement (if picking up a cat in a trap, they must also be TNR certified). Click here to sign the release. If you know another rescuer needs to pick up for you, please be sure the release is signed at least one day prior to the appointment.
